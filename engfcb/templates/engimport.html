Import  Declaration

It refers to a series of procedures for bringing foreign goods into the country under the Customs Act.
For import customs clearance, the import goods must meet the requirements for permission, approval, indication, or other conditions as prescribed by relevant laws and regulations.
Before the goods are brought in, we will quickly and accurately check the HS code consistency review, import requirements,  and import declaration documents to reduce unnecessary customs clearance time.
